# Inner Circle UGC Accelerator — Course Modules

This is the full module list for the Inner Circle UGC Accelerator at No More Mondays. The Student Reply skill uses this to point students to the right module when answering their questions. Upload this file to your Project Knowledge alongside your Role Project so the skill can search it.

**Course summary:** The Inner Circle UGC Accelerator is a high-touch coaching program that helps creators monetize content creation as a UGC creator without needing a large following. Three months long. 29 modules total across 3 phases.

---

## Month 1: UGC Foundation

1. **Don't wait, just START (Mindset)** — Learn the mindset of taking action before you are ready.
2. **Time Management Mastery** — A system for managing your time and capacity.
3. **Monetization Roadmap** — Your path to earning as a UGC content creator.
4. **UGC Profile Design** — Setting up your social profiles to position you correctly.
5. **UGC Video Types** — The various types and formats of UGC videos.
6. **UGC Content Reframe** — Foundational strategy lesson for finding inspiration for videos already proven to work with real numbers.
7. **Editing Videos** — Learn how to edit videos on CapCut.
8. **Marketing For Creators** — Learn the strategy of marketing and human psychology as a content creator.
9. **UGC Portfolio Pro** — Set up your UGC Portfolio.
10. **Find UGC Job Opportunities** — Where to find UGC opportunities.
11. **Outreach Mastery Part 1** — Setting up for your first round of brand pitches.
12. **Outreach Mastery Part 2** — Sending your pitches with our templates.
13. **The No-Brainer Offer** — Our go-to offer to start with for your first brand deals.
14. **Setting Rates & Pricing** — Deeper dive into how pricing works in the UGC industry.
15. **Offer Mastery** — Learn how to set up and craft your specific offer.
16. **Negotiation Mastery** — Learn how to negotiate with brands.
17. **Recap + Next Steps** — Month 1 wrap-up.

## Month 2: Automation System OS

1. **Welcome - Automation System** — Getting ready to set up your automated pitching system.
2. **Onbento - Email Automation Strategy** — Step-by-step guide for setting up your first campaigns in Onbento. (Platform may change soon.)
3. **Master Onbento (team Q&A)** — Helpful lesson with additional information for setting up Onbento.
4. **NMM Outreach Strategy** — Advanced outreach strategy for manually pitching your dream brands.
5. **Keep It Simple (Mindset)** — How to keep the mindset of not overthinking things and staying in motion.
6. **Your First Brand Call** — How to lead a successful discovery call with a brand that establishes you as a strategist.
7. **Overdeliver, Every Time** — Mindset to adopt in how you deliver for clients.
8. **Retainers (How To Land Them)** — How to pitch retainers to brands you have previously worked with.
9. **Your Game Plan** — Recap lesson of everything you need to accomplish in Month 2.

## Month 3: Winning Strategy OS

1. **The Winning Strategy Review** — Walkthrough for how to review your results from Month 2 outreach and how to improve them.
2. **Niche Domination** — Advanced strategy for going deeper in your niche of choice.
3. **Thank You + Next Steps** — Wrap up and next steps for you.

## How the Student Reply skill uses this file

When a student asks a question, the Student Reply skill:

1. Searches the NMM FAQ database in Notion for the closest-matching answer
2. Cross-references the question against this module list to identify which module the student is on or stuck on
3. Drafts a reply that points to the specific module (e.g. "this is covered in Module 12, Outreach Mastery Part 2") so the student can self-serve

If you remove or edit this file, the skill loses its ability to route students to specific modules and will only return generic answers from the FAQ.

## Updating this file

When the course curriculum changes (new modules added, modules removed or renamed), Albert updates this file in the canonical `nmm-claude-os` repo, posts in the team Slack channel, and team members re-download and re-upload to their Project Knowledge.
